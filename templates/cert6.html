Bachelor of Business Management with Marketing

This certificate acknowledges the successful completion of the Bachelor of Business Management with Marketing program at the University of the West of England. The coursework combined core business disciplines with specialised marketing knowledge, covering topics such as consumer behaviour, brand strategy, digital marketing, and global business management.

Through real-world projects, internships, and industry engagement, the program emphasized practical application of business and marketing strategies. Graduates are equipped with the skills and insights needed to develop creative marketing solutions, manage business operations effectively, and contribute to organizational growth in a competitive, global marketplace.
